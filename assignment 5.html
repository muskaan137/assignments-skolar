My Top 5 Favorite Bollywood Movies

  1. humty sharma ki dulhania
    • Year: 2018
    • Main Actors: varun dhawan, alia bhatt
    • Description: a comedy romance movie .
  2. Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge
    • Year: 1995
    • Main Actors: Shah Rukh Khan, Kajol, Amrish Puri
    • Description: A romantic drama about a young couple who fall in love during a trip to Europe, but must overcome familial and cultural barriers to be together.
  3. Lagaan
    • Year: 2001
    • Main Actors: Aamir Khan, Gracy Singh, Raj Zutshi
    • Description: Set in the 19th century, a group of villagers challenge British colonists to a game of cricket to avoid paying a high tax.
  4. 3 Idiots
    • Year: 2009
    • Main Actors: Aamir Khan, Kareena Kapoor, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi
    • Description: A comedy-drama that explores the education system in India through the journey of three engineering students and their pursuit of their dreams.
  5. Dangal
    • Year: 2016
    • Main Actors: Aamir Khan, Sakshi Tanwar, Fatima Sana Shaikh
    • Description: A biographical sports drama based on the true story of wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat and his struggle to train his daughters in wrestling against societal norms.
